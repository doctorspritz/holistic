Is Holistica Health a legitimate Australian telehealth clinic?

Yes. Holistica Health is an Australian telehealth clinic providing clinician-led care by our AHPRA-registered doctors. We follow Medical Board of Australia telehealth guidelines, Australian privacy requirements, and strict clinical governance to ensure safe, professional online GP consultations.

  • Verify our doctors on the AHPRA Register
  • Read the Medical Board of Australia telehealth guidelines

What sets Holistica Health apart in the Telehealth industry?

Holistica Health is a specialised GP telehealth clinic offering clinician-led care 7 days a week for metro and remote regions. Our AHPRA-registered GPs, including credentialed dermatology and ADHD treatment focused doctors, provide targeted support for medical weight loss, hair loss, metabolic health, and skin concerns. We bridge the gap between standard GP care and long specialist waitlists, delivering fast and evidence-based telehealth consultations across Australia.

What is the cost of a Holistica Health consultation?

Holistica Health offers transparent private telehealth pricing, with affordable consultation fees typically ranging from $59 to $350, depending on the service and complexity. Your fee includes a comprehensive assessment by an AHPRA-registered GP and may include pathology, ECG, specialist referrals and a personalised treatment plan where clinically appropriate.

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

No. You do not need a referral to book with Holistica Health. New patients can book directly and complete a clinical pre-screening form, which helps our AHPRA-registered GPs assess eligibility and safety before your consultation. This is especially important for specialised GP services such as ADHD support, medical weight loss, and natural medicine.
