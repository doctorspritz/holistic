MEET YOUR DOCTOR

Dr. Mehdi

Dr Mehdi founded LifeMed Clinic with a mission to help people make the right lifestyle choices to achieve a greater and healthier lifespan.

 

Having practiced medicine for over 19 years, Dr Mehdi came to realise an increased demand for a holistic treatment approach that not only focuses on a patient's presenting problems, but also the lifestyle routines underpinning those problems.

Learn more

19 Years of Medicine Practice to Help Enhance Your Wellbeing

LifeMed clinic is a medical practice specialising in modern conventional medicine including evidence-based lifestyle therapeutic interventions and natural treatment options.

 

We take a collaborative approach to your care. It all starts with the lifestyle choices that you are willing to make to improve your health and wellbeing. We will meet you where you are and hold your hand throughout the journey of changing your lifestyle habits.     

 

Our practice is based on conventional medical sciences and is not affiliated with integrative, alternative or functional medicine.

Conditions We Can Help With

Chronic Pain

Therapeutic natural medicines and lifestyle interventions have shown to be effective in managing and sometimes reversing symptoms associated with chronic conditions such chronic pain, migraines, multiple sclerosis, endometriosis, menopause, arthritis, insomnia and PTSD.

Learn More

Gut Health

The gut-brain connection goes both ways. Studies show a direct connection between gut health and mental wellbeing. LifeMed’s philosophy is to integrate these findings with conventional medicine guidelines to adopt a holistic treatment.

Learn More

Autoimmune Diseases

Adopting an anti-inflammatory dietary routine and other lifestyle modifications can alleviate symptoms associated with autoimmune diseases such as multiple sclerosis (MS), Rheumatoid arthritis (RA), psoriasis, and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD).

Learn More

Fatigue and Depression

Depression and fatigue can significantly disable the quality of life and productivity of the individual. While many factors play a role in the development of depression and fatigue, both have been associated with increased inflammation in the immune system which is often caused by lifestyle and environmental factors.

Learn More

Sleep Disorders

Recent data shows that 4 in every 10 Australians frequently suffer from impaired sleep. Research has shown that a combination of lifestyle medicine strategies and medically approved natural medicines can effectively enhance the quality of sleep.

Learn More

Diabetes and Pre-Diabetes

An overwhelming body of scientific research supports the fundamental impact of lifestyle medicine modalities in both the prevention and management of pre-diabetes and diabetes.

Learn More

Anxiety and Stress

About 25% of our population grapple with anxiety. Though there are various factors at play, recent studies have shown a strong link between diet and emotional wellbeing.

Learn More

Weight Loss

Overweight and Obesity are chronic health problems with a lifestyle aetiology. Unfortunately, there is no quick and easy way for treating these conditions. It takes motivation, compliance and big lifestyle changes to achieve and maintain an optimal weight.

Learn More
